Frequently Asked Questions

The food tracking application is a tool that allows individuals to track and monitor their intake of nutrients like proteins, fats, carbohydrates, and macronutrients like potassium, iron, Sodium, etc. The application provides information about intolerances, recipes, and ingredients in food items, and helps users make informed decisions about their diet for a healthy lifestyle.

The food tracking application takes manual input from user and voice inputs to track food consumed by individuals. Information about nutrients is then extracted based on that food item. The app provides daily insight into the nutrient intake of individuals and makes suggestions on food items to be consumed to meet daily requirements.

The application makes use of relational databases like MySQL to store data and perform complex operations with efficiency. The text translation of voice input represents unstructured data and is stored in a NoSQL database, while the useful data obtained about the users diet is transferred to a relational database.

The application provides insight into the nutrient intake of individuals on a daily basis and makes suggestions on food items to be consumed to meet daily requirements. By monitoring their diet, users can prevent deficiency diseases that cause degradation of life and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

The application educates users on the importance of diet in a healthy lifestyle by providing information about the daily nutrient requirements and the amounts consumed. This helps users make informed decisions about their diet and prevent nutrient deficiencies.
